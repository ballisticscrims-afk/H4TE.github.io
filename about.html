ABOUT H4TE

H4TE GAMING is a competitive esports organization dedicated to excellence across all FPS game modes. Built by players, for players, we compete with purpose, discipline, and an unbreakable team mindset.

Success at H4TE is more than raw skill. Communication, strategy, and trust define how we operate. Every member is held to the highest standard of professionalism—on and off the server.

We are always growing. Whether you're a seasoned competitor or a rising talent hungry to improve, there is a place for you here.
