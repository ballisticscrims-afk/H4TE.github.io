ABOUT H4TE

H4TE GAMING is a competitive esports organization dedicated to excellence across all FPS game modes. Built by players, for players, we compete with purpose, discipline, and an unbreakable team mindset. Our focus is not limited to one title or playstyle—we adapt, evolve, and perform across multiple FPS platforms and competitive environments.

At H4TE GAMING, we believe success comes from more than individual skill. Communication, strategy, and trust are the foundation of everything we do. Every member is held to a high standard of professionalism, respect, and commitment, both in and out of competition.

We are always looking to grow our roster and welcome new talent—whether you are an experienced competitor, a rising player hungry to improve, or a dedicated team-oriented gamer ready to put in the work. If you have passion, consistency, and the drive to compete, there is a place for you here.

More than just an esports team, H4TE GAMING is a family. We support one another, push each other to be better, and create an environment where everyone can grow. Wins matter—but loyalty, respect, and unity matter more.

Join us. Compete with us. Grow with us.
